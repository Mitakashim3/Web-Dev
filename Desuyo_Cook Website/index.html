How to Cook Rice



Cooking rice is a simple yet essential skill in the kitchen. Here's how to cook perfect rice every time:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of rice (white or brown)
  • 1 3/4 cups of water or broth
  • Pinch of salt (optional)


Instructions:


  1. Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear. This removes excess starch and helps prevent the rice from becoming sticky.
  2. In a pot, combine the rinsed rice, water or broth, and salt if using. Bring to a boil over high heat.
  3. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low and cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid.
  4. Simmer the rice for 18-20 minutes for white rice or 30-40 minutes for brown rice.
  5. Remove the pot from the heat and let it sit, covered, for 5-10 minutes to allow the rice to steam and finish cooking.
  6. Fluff the rice with a fork before serving.

Enjoy your perfectly cooked rice as a side dish or as a base for your favorite stir-fries and curries!
